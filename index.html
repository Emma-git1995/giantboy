About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival action game where your objective is simple but challenging: stay alive as long as possible while battling endless waves of monsters. As you progress, the difficulty increases, testing your skills and strategic thinking.

Your character automatically attacks nearby enemies, allowing you to focus on movement and positioning. The game's addictive gameplay loop will keep you coming back for more as you try to beat your previous survival time.

Key Features

Auto-Combat System

Focus on movement while your character automatically attacks nearby enemies, creating fast-paced and strategic gameplay.

Character Progression

Level up and unlock powerful upgrades and abilities that enhance your combat potential as you survive longer.

Diverse Enemies

Battle against a variety of monster types with unique attack patterns and behaviors that require different strategies.

Special Abilities

Unlock and master powerful special abilities that can clear the screen of enemies or provide crucial survivability.

Time-Based Challenges

The longer you survive, the more challenging the game becomes, with stronger enemies and more intense waves.

Simple Controls

Easy to pick up but hard to master controls make the game accessible for players of all skill levels.

How to Play

  1. Movement: Use WASD or arrow keys (on desktop) or the on-screen joystick (on mobile) to move your character around the map.
  2. Combat: Your character will automatically attack nearby enemies. Focus on positioning to maximize your effectiveness.
  3. Leveling Up: Defeat enemies to gain experience. When you level up, choose from available upgrades to enhance your abilities.
  4. Special Attacks: Special abilities have cooldowns. Use them strategically to clear waves of enemies or escape dangerous situations.
  5. Survive: Your goal is to survive as long as possible. The game gets progressively harder, so stay alert and keep moving!
